Problem III – Some business rules
• R1 – A student always has a number and name
• R2 – Students’ number is always a 7 digits number (e.g. 1190001)
• R3 – Length of a students’ name cannot be shorter than 5 chars 
• R4 – Students’ grade ranges between 0 and 20 (no decimals)
• R5 – Students’ grade results from an evaluatation process (which is out of 
scope in this application, but only occurs once)
